## OneMIKA — Procrastination Spotted

Made some progress on the remaining workflows — we're approaching the end of the main development phase, and the next step is setting up the final Excel file to create the data for user testing. But I was procrastinating. Again.

I'm calling it what it is: procrastination is the comfortable exit. It's easier to come up with an excuse — even a lie — than to sit with the tedious task. And when those avoidances pile up, they turn into energy vampires. The weight of undone things drains more than the things themselves ever would. I want to control this better. Today I recognized the pattern. Tomorrow I act on it — Claude recommended starting the day with the thing I've been avoiding, and I plan to do that.
