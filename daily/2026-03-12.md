# Journal — 2026-03-12 (Thursday)

## One Word: Resolved

Second day of the KNIME Level 1 training in Munich. Started with a nice hotel breakfast and got to the office campus early — so early that nobody was there yet, which gave me time to check a few emails in peace.

The training went well. Same critical voices as the day before, but this time it didn't get to me. Yesterday's reflection helped — I went in with a more relaxed stance. When points came up, I acknowledged them and moved on. "Here's how I'd approach it — that's it." No defensiveness, no internal frustration. Just calm confidence. By the end, everyone seemed genuinely satisfied. They gave a round of applause and said it was lightweight and entertaining. That's exactly what I'm going for.

One useful piece of feedback: we had a lot of time left. We'd planned until 12:00 and were basically done by 10:00–10:30. So the idea is to shift more content into day two and end a little earlier on day one. Simple, practical, and it makes total sense. Will adjust the curriculum.

The other big thing was a conversation with the project manager about OneMika. We dug into the data issues I'd discovered, and it turns out the missing data points and errors are a grey area — hard to pin on any one side. But I took a clear lesson from it: for our Low Code Service, we need a **data point inventory** as the very first step of any new project. "Which data points do we have? Which do we need? Where do they come from?" Calculate that into the project plan upfront. There's a small upcoming project with colleagues where we can test this approach already.

Left the Siemens campus around 12:30–13:00 — about four hours earlier than expected. Got home early, saw the family, had a lovely relaxed evening. Did some sauna, which was exactly what I needed. Then fell asleep on the living room couch around 10, woke up at midnight, and shuffled off to bed. Not exactly quality sleep, but the evening itself was gold.

One thing I completely forgot: the church choir performance is on Sunday, and I'd planned to practice this week. Didn't happen in Munich. Need to squeeze that in today and tomorrow. Feeling confident though — three songs, moderate difficulty, we've rehearsed them several times. Looking forward to it.
