Number one, right out of the gate. Also used an AI assistant to write a proper biography and headline — mine probably kicks harder than what the other founders wrote. Not bragging, just observing.

The community goes live end of May. Three weeks. I was expecting this to feel like a chore. Instead, it was the most engaging part of the day. It reignited something — a pull toward re-engaging more deeply with the KNIME community, not out of nostalgia but because there's real strategic value.
