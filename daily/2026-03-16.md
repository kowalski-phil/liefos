## One Word: Engaged

A day of contrasts — Siemens frustrations, crypto curiosity, parenting pride and challenge. But the through-line was engagement. I was mentally in it all day.

**OneMika project** is getting tense, and I mean that in a good way. This is the customer-facing work I wanted when I moved to GBS. The data lineage issue was maddening though — a beautifully structured lineage report where every field is mapped out with calculations and baselines, but when you open the actual data set in Excel, not a single field name matches. 212 usable fields identified by the AI, only 5–6 gaps.
