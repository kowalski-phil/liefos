## 3. EU AI Act -- August 2, 2026 Deadline for High-Risk AI Systems

**Why it's trending:**
The Annex III obligations become enforceable August 2, 2026 -- less than 3 months away. Financial services firms using AI in creditworthiness assessment, insurance pricing, or any system that classifies the financial standing of individuals are in scope with no transition relief. Penalties: up to 35M EUR or 7% of worldwide turnover.

**Finance relevance:**
Any Siemens-scale enterprise using AI in procurement scoring, vendor risk rating, or financial analytics may be touching high-risk AI territory. The compliance requirements are concrete: documented AI inventory, human oversight mechanisms, automatic inference logging, bias monitoring thresholds, and named accountability (SM&CR-style). This is a CFO and CPO conversation, not just a legal one.

**Confidence: HIGH** -- Finextra, KNIME's own compliance blog, multiple regulatory sources

**Post hook angle:**
"August 2 is 81 days away. If you run AI in procurement scoring or vendor risk assessment and haven't done an Annex III gap analysis, you are not ready."

## 5. LinkedIn Algorithm May 2026 -- Document Posts Are Winning, Carousels Are Dead
