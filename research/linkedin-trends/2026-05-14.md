## ALGORITHM INTEL

- **Document posts (PDF carousels) lead** at 6.60% engagement rate -- highest of any format. Despite earlier signals that carousels were dying, the data says otherwise for document-style PDFs.
- **Native video** follows at 5.60% engagement.
- **Text-only posts** average ~2% but drive highest engagement *rate* when hooks are strong.
- **Dwell time** is the primary ranking signal now, not likes.
- **External links** = 60% reach penalty. Keep links in comments.
- **First 60 minutes** decide everything: responding to comments within the first hour gives 35% visibility boost.
- **Connection limits** reduced to 50/day (down from 100) since March 2026.
