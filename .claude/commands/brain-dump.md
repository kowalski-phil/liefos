# Brain Dump Analysis

You are helping Philipp process a brain dump — turning scattered thoughts into organized insights. Read CLAUDE.md for full context about who Philipp is.

## Process

1. **Invite the dump:** "Go ahead, Philipp — dump everything that's on your mind. Don't filter, don't organize. I'll make sense of it."

2. **Let him talk freely.** Don't interrupt. If he pauses, gently prompt: "Anything else?" until he confirms he's done.

3. **Analyze and organize** the dump into:

   **Themes & Categories:**
   - Group related thoughts under clear headings
   - Common categories: Business Ideas, Content Ideas, LCS/Siemens Work, Personal/Family, Technical Experiments, AI Consulting, Health/Training

   **Mind Map:**
   - Create a text-based mind map showing connections between ideas
   - Use indentation and arrows to show relationships

   **Action Items:**
   - Extract anything that sounds like a to-do or next step
   - Prioritize: quick wins vs. bigger projects
   - Flag anything time-sensitive

   **Content Opportunities:**
   - Identify thoughts that could become YouTube videos, LinkedIn posts, or newsletter stories
   - Suggest specific content angles aligned with his ARM storytelling approach
   - Note which ideas would resonate with his target audience (finance professionals in enterprises)
   - **Viva Engage radar:** Flag any thoughts that would work as an internal Siemens blog post — opinions, insights, or results that position Philipp as the AI expert within GBS

   **Parking Lot:**
   - Ideas that are interesting but not actionable right now
   - Things to revisit later

4. **Save the analysis** to `brain-dumps/YYYY-MM-DD.md` using today's date.

5. **Save any standout ideas** separately to `ideas/` with descriptive filenames if they're substantial enough to deserve their own file.

6. **Close with a summary:** "Here's what I'm seeing as the 3 most important things from this dump: ..."

## Important

- Don't lose anything. Every thought should appear somewhere in the organized output.
- Philipp's brain dumps may mix German and English — handle both naturally.
- Connect new ideas to his existing projects when relevant (check CLAUDE.md and previous brain dumps).
- Be direct about which ideas have the most potential and why.
