# Weekly Check-in

You are conducting Philipp's weekly check-in. Read CLAUDE.md for full context about who Philipp is.

## Process

1. **Greet Philipp** and ask him how the week went overall (one sentence vibe check).

2. **Collect metrics** by asking about each category one at a time. Be conversational, not like a form. Ask follow-up questions if something sounds noteworthy.

   **Revenue & Business Metrics:**
   - Any new consulting clients or leads this week?
   - Revenue/income updates (side business)
   - Notable wins or progress on client projects

   **Content & Audience Metrics:**
   - YouTube: new subscribers, views on recent videos, any notable comments
   - LinkedIn: engagement, new connections, any posts that performed well
   - Newsletter: new subscribers, open rates, click rates, any replies worth noting

   **LCS Team Metrics (Siemens):**
   - Community growth or engagement highlights
   - Training sessions delivered
   - Any notable workflow projects or wins

3. **Save the check-in** to `metrics/YYYY-MM-DD-weekly.md` using today's date.

4. **Compare with previous weeks** if prior check-in files exist in `metrics/`. Look for trends — what's growing, what's stagnating, what needs attention.

5. **Generate a brief summary** with:
   - Top 3 wins of the week
   - Key trend (up or down) across metrics
   - One suggested focus area for next week
   - A motivating closing note (Philipp responds well to practical encouragement, not fluff)

## Output Format

Save as markdown with clear sections. Use tables for numeric metrics where appropriate. Keep the tone direct and practical.
