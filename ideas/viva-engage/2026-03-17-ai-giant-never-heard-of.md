## The AI Giant You've Probably Never Heard Of

Last week, I got Claude Code running on my Siemens machine.

Not through a workaround. Not through some shadow IT hack. Through our official LLM gateway — fully compliant, fully supported.

I posted the setup steps in an internal AI community. Within five minutes, people were asking "How did you do that?" and "Can you show me?"

That reaction told me something. Most of us don't even know this tool exists.

### "Wait — Who's Anthropic?"

If you've heard of AI, you've heard of ChatGPT. Fair enough. OpenAI has the brand recognition.

But here's the thing.

Among companies buying AI services for the first time, Anthropic — the company behind Claude — now wins 70% of head-to-head matchups against OpenAI. 70% of Fortune 100 companies use Claude. Their revenue went from $1 billion to $14 billion in just over a year.

This isn't the scrappy underdog anymore. This is the enterprise standard — and most people outside of tech circles haven't noticed the shift.

### Why Should You Care?

Because Claude is available to us. Right now. Through the Siemens LLM gateway.

And it's not just a chatbot. Claude Code is an AI that works alongside you in your actual workflow — reading your files, analyzing your data, building solutions with you. Not in a browser window. In your working environment.

I used it to analyze a 35-sheet Excel file, map 212 data fields across systems, and draft structured project documentation. Tasks that would have taken me days took minutes.

This isn't about one tool being "better" than another. It's about knowing what's available to you — and actually using it.

The biggest gap in AI adoption at Siemens isn't access. It's awareness.

**So here's my question:** Did you know Claude was available through our LLM gateway? And if not — what other tools are we sitting on without realizing it?

*This is my personal perspective based on my own experience. Your mileage may vary — but I'd encourage you to explore.*
