# We're in the 90%. Are You?

Gartner says 90% of finance functions will deploy at least one AI-enabled technology by the end of 2026.

That's not a prediction anymore. That's a deadline.

## What It Looks Like When You're Already There

In our GBS Low Code Service team, we're not just "using AI." We built a workflow in n8n where the AI improves its own prompts after every single run.

Let that sink in.

Every time the workflow executes, it evaluates its own output, identifies what could be better, and rewrites the instructions for the next round. No manual tuning. No one sitting there tweaking parameters.

It gets better while we sleep.

### This Isn't Science Fiction

This is a production workflow. Running today. Built with tools available to every Siemens employee — n8n, an AI model, and a bit of creativity.

The first version was decent. The tenth version was good. The fiftieth version? It outperforms what we could have written manually — because it's learned from patterns we wouldn't even have noticed.

That's what "deploying AI" actually looks like. Not a chatbot answering FAQs. Not a fancy demo in a town hall. A system that compounds its own intelligence over time.

### The Door Is Open

If you're one of the curious ones — the kind of person who reads a stat like "90%" and thinks *where do I start?* — our GBS Low Code Service knowledge board is exactly that starting point. KNIME. n8n. RapidMiner. Real tools, real use cases, built by colleagues who use them every day.

You don't need to be a developer. You don't need permission from IT. You need curiosity and a Wednesday afternoon.

**Gartner says 90% will deploy AI by end of 2026. The real question isn't whether your team will be part of that number. It's whether you'll be the one who made it happen — or the one who watched it happen.**
