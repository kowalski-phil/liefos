# Internal Blog — Siemens WordPress

## The Idea

Use the Siemens-hosted WordPress blog as an internal personal blog to build visibility beyond the LCS community. Linked in email signature and Microsoft Teams status. Conversational, opinionated, personal. Not corporate comms. A person with a perspective.

## Why It Matters

- Reaches the broader Siemens population (~300,000) outside the existing 500+ LCS community
- Directly serves the strategic goal: becoming the AI expert within GBS, not just "Mr. KNIME"
- Low barrier to entry — test with a few posts before committing
- Internal visibility compounds: people share, tag, forward
- WordPress supports scheduled posts — can batch-create and drip out over time

## Content Guidelines

- Keep posts conversational — write like you're explaining to a smart colleague over coffee
- 300-500 words max
- One clear idea per post
- End with a question to invite discussion
- Always include: 1 featured image idea + 1-2 in-post image ideas
- Personal opinion disclaimer where needed, but don't apologize for having an opinion
- Be opinionated about tech and ways of working — but steer clear of geopolitics and anything that could alienate colleagues in specific regions
