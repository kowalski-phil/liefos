# Custom Weekly Newsletter for Clients

Send a curated newsletter every week to each client's department covering everything they care about — Claude Code updates, relevant AI news, tips specific to their use cases.

From Mark's video: "I'll send a custom newsletter to your department every week with everything that I know you care about. Claude Code. Jump in."

## Why this is interesting
- Adds recurring value on top of training engagements
- Keeps you top-of-mind between sessions
- Low effort if automated (Phil already has newsletter workflows in n8n)
- Differentiator: not generic AI news, but curated for THEIR stack and THEIR pain points

## How it could work
- n8n workflow that pulls from curated sources based on client profile
- Claude generates a short, personalized briefing
- Sent weekly — keeps the retainer feeling worth it
- Could reuse patterns from Phil's own `/daily-brief` command

## Connection
- Phil already runs Bytes & Budgets newsletter (ARM method)
- Has n8n infrastructure for content automation
- This is the B2B version of what he already does for himself
