# YouTube Video Idea: Trump Banned My AI Tool

**Date:** 2026-03-05
**Type:** YouTube Video
**Status:** Idea

## Title Options

- "Trump Banned This AI Tool. I Use It Every Day."
- "The AI Tool Trump Doesn't Want You to Use (I Built My Business On It)"
- "Trump Blacklisted My Favorite AI. Here's What It Can Actually Do."

## Core Narrative

Use the Anthropic/Pentagon blacklist as the hook, then pivot into a practical demo showing how Claude + n8n work together for real business automation. End by reframing the Gartner "could" into "will."

## Structure

### 1. Cold Open — Trump Hook
Trump blacklists Anthropic/Claude from government use. Shock value, real news, gets attention.

### 2. Social Proof Stack (30 seconds)
- Claude dethrones ChatGPT as #1 iPhone app after the ban
- Chinese AI labs (DeepSeek, Moonshot, MiniMax) caught creating 24,000 fake accounts to steal from Claude
- "So what is this tool that the government fears, the public loves, and competitors are literally stealing from?"

### 3. What Claude Actually Is
Brief intro to Claude / Claude Code / Anthropic. Position it as the thinking layer.

### 4. "But Claude Alone Isn't Enough" — Pivot to n8n
- The key framework: **KNIME = data. n8n = processes. Claude = thinking.**
- n8n makes AI access visual and lowers entry barriers
- n8n is the bridge between AI models and business processes
- Without n8n, Claude is powerful but locked behind a terminal. With n8n, a finance colleague can trigger AI workflows without code.
- Counter the "is low-code dead?" narrative: low-code is the interface layer that makes AI usable for non-developers

### 5. Live Demo
Show a real workflow — Claude + n8n working together. Options:
- YouTube extraction pipeline
- Excel formula analysis (35 sheets, 200K+ formulas)
- Another practical business example

### 6. The Gartner Close
"Gartner says advanced ERPs *could* cut financial close times by 30%. Could. Let me show you how they *will*."
Reframe from analyst talk to practitioner reality.

## Target Audience
Finance professionals in medium-to-large enterprises — accountants, procurement specialists, business analysts who are being told from the top to adopt AI (54% of CFOs prioritizing AI agents in 2026) but don't know where to start.

## Positioning
Not "Mr. KNIME" — the AI automation guy for finance. The person who bridges AI hype and business reality.
