# Freemium AI Training Funnel

Mirror the Siemens model for the side business: **paid workshops** (prompting, Claude Code, etc.) funded by **free content** that demonstrates expertise.

## The Model

- **Free on LinkedIn:** Short snippets from "Prompting That Works" — quick tips, one-minute videos, bite-sized value
- **LinkedIn short videos:** Under 60 seconds, native format — perfect for prompting tips and quick AI demos
- **Paid offering:** Full workshops (prompting, Claude Code, possibly more)
- **Newsletter tie-in:** ARM storytelling emails build trust → readers see LinkedIn snippets as proof of expertise → paid workshop becomes the natural next step

## Why This Works

- LinkedIn content solves the declining impressions problem (two weeks of decline)
- Prompting course modules are already built — repurposing, not creating from scratch
- Free content establishes authority; paid workshops monetize it
- Newsletter (ARM method) handles the trust-building middle of the funnel
- Same content serves both Siemens (internal) and side business (external)
